About Moa

Biography

Hello! I'm Moa, a passionate UX designer with a strong commitment to creating meaningful digital experiences. Currently, I work at NIBE AB, where I focus on IT and digitalization projects that aim to enhance user interactions and streamline processes.

Holding a bachelor's degree in informatics, I have developed a comprehensive skill set in UX design that includes user research, requirements gathering, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. My experience within UX and UI has allowed me to tackle diverse projects, each presenting its own unique challenges. I thrive in fast-paced environments that encourage innovation and creativity, and I'm dedicated to developing solutions that not only meet user needs but also exceed expectations.

My approach to UX design is deeply rooted in empathy and curiosity. I am fascinated by how people interact with technology and the ways in which digital experiences can improve their lives. This drive motivates me to create accessible and user-friendly solutions that are intuitive and enjoyable. I believe that design should not only solve problems but also enhance overall satisfaction.

Right now I'm on a journey to learn web development, I've just finished my indepentent course in Javascript, now with the goal to combine this new knowledge with my work in UX & UI design.

Interests

In my personal life, I enjoy exploring new ideas, engaging in creative pursuits, and collaborating with others. I'm always eager to learn and grow, whether through professional development opportunities or new hobbies. I spend a lot of my sparetime painting, while listening to some of my favourite music.
