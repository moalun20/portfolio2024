About Moa

Biography

Hi there! I'm Moa Lundgren, a curious and detail-loving UX designer with a background in informatics and a passion for creating meaningful digital experiences. Over the years, I've honed my skills in UX design, from user research and requirements gathering to wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing.

Each project I've worked on has brought its own unique challenges, and that's exactly what I love about this field. Tackling complexity with creativity and seeing a solution come to life is endlessly rewarding. Whether it's finding a clever way to improve usability or turning a spark of an idea into a polished product, I'm all in.

My approach to UX design is deeply rooted in empathy and curiosity. I am fascinated by how people interact with technology and the ways in which digital experiences can improve their lives. This drive motivates me to create accessible and user-friendly solutions that are intuitive and enjoyable. I believe that design should not only solve problems but also enhance overall satisfaction.

I'm at my best in environments where collaboration is key, and where different perspectives spark new ideas. I believe the most impactful designs come from combining curiosity with creativity, always striving to create solutions that not only work but leave a lasting, positive impression on the people who use them.

Recently, my curiosity led me to dive into the world of web development. After completing a course in JavaScript, I'm excited to bring this newfound technical knowledge into my UX and UI design work, opening up new ways to create thoughtful and innovative solutions.

Interests

In my personal life, I enjoy exploring new ideas, engaging in creative pursuits, and collaborating with others. Beyond design, I find inspiration in all things creative. Lately, I've been diving into Procreate and can't get enough of creating patterns, it's my new obsession! Painting and sketching are my go-to ways to unwind and explore new ideas.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to see some of my work. ICollaboration, curiosity, and a drive to keep learning define who I am. If you're curious about my work or want to discuss design and finding smart solutions to challenges, feel free to reach out!
